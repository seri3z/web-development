Hello!
The general course of the English language for foreigners forms communication skills in everyday life situations, includes teaching correct pronunciation, grammar and vocabulary. Much attention is paid to the practice of oral and written speech, listening comprehension, and reading. You learn at different levels of difficulty.
Key components in the language learning process:
Vocabulary
App: English Vocabulary Builder (DK English for Everyone) Book: English Grammar in USA. Fourth edition Book with answers and Interactive eBook Book: English Collocations in Use (Cambridge)
Grammar
Book: Oxford Advanced Learner’s Dictionary (OALD) Book: English Grammar in Use (Cambridge) Book: Practical English Usage (Oxford)
Communication
Book: English Grammar: Understanding the Basics (Cambridge) Book: The Official Cambridge Guide to IELTS Book: Grammar for IELTS (Cambridge University Press)
